Where it all began

Commencement — The Spark

My passion for programming began during my school years at Nalanda College, Colombo 10 , where I was first introduced to the fascinating world of computers. I studied ICT for both my Ordinary Level (O/L) and Advanced Level (A/L) examinations, achieving good marks in both. These studies gave me a strong foundation in various ICT concepts such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, MySQL, computer systems, software applications, networking, pseudo-code, algorithms, and the history of ICT.

Through this learning, I developed a deep understanding of how websites and web applications are structured and styled, how logic and interactivity are implemented, how databases and networks operate, and how different components of a computer system function together. I also learned the fundamentals of programming logic, problem-solving using algorithms, and how to plan solutions using pseudocode.

What truly ignited my enthusiasm was my active role in the Computer Society of Nalanda College (CSNC) , where I organized tech events, participated in coding competitions, and mentored peers. This experience not only developed my technical skills but also helped me grow as a leader and team player, giving me the confidence and drive to pursue a professional career in software engineering.

Through practical assignments, lab sessions, group projects, and real-world simulations, I was able to apply what I learned in the classroom to build functional applications and solve complex problems, which significantly enhanced my technical and analytical skills. As part of my university coursework, I also developed several fully functional e-commerce applications, m-commerce applications, and desktop applications that demonstrated my ability to apply theoretical knowledge to real-world software solutions.

Professional chapter

Career & Growth

Professionally, I have gained two years of experience as a full-stack developer at XdotO Concepts Pte Limited (International Plaza, Singapore), having joined as an intern and quickly advanced to a Software Engineer role, developing robust skills in both backend and frontend technologies. During my time there, I contributed to several key projects, including Melioraa (an Industry 4.0 Machine Monitoring System), Dialog Power App (an Alarms and Notification Mobile Application), and Morex (a Process Monitoring System).

I designed and developed scalable web applications using Java, Spring Boot, ReactJS, VueJS, PHP, GO, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, Material-UI, Grafana, JUnit, MySQL, Hibernate, JPA, Axios, JSON, and Jasper Reports. I built and maintained RESTful APIs, worked with microservices architecture, and implemented micro-frontend solutions. My responsibilities also included Dockerizing applications, managing CI/CD pipelines, and handling deployments with AWS. I worked in Agile teams, used Jira for tracking, Git(GitHub, GitLab) for version control, and Postman/Swagger for API testing and documentation. I also developed leadership skills by leading small teams, handling planning, delegation, and reviews. This role enhanced my communication, problem-solving, and collaboration capabilities.

In addition to my full-time role, I have passionately pursued freelance projects that allowed me to explore and apply a wide range of technologies beyond the scope of my main job. Driven by curiosity and a love for building meaningful solutions, I took on several freelance assignments where I independently learned and implemented Python, Django, FastAPI, and MongoDB. These projects not only sharpened my technical skills but also deepened my problem-solving abilities, strengthened my self-discipline, and fueled my continuous learning mindset—turning my passion into tangible results.
