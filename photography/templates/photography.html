Where it all began

Commencement — The Eye Opens

Photography has always been one of my innate talents — something that came naturally to me from a very young age. My journey began in childhood, not with expensive cameras or formal training, but with a simple mobile phone. I was fascinated by the ability to freeze a moment in time, to tell stories through visuals, and to see the world through a different lens. Even as a child, I found myself drawn to the small details — the way light fell across a surface, the symmetry in nature, or the emotions captured in a candid smile.

My passion for photography began to take a more defined shape during my school years at Nalanda College, where I was deeply inspired by the Nalanda College Photographic Art Society (NPAS) . The work produced by the members, as well as the creative atmosphere they fostered, gave me a sense of direction. It opened my eyes to the artistic side of photography and helped me understand that it was more than just clicking a button—it was about composition, emotion, timing, and storytelling.

Beyond school, I found further inspiration from both local and international photographers on social media platforms. I would spend hours studying their work, learning different techniques, experimenting with styles, and absorbing knowledge from various online communities. Their creativity pushed me to keep improving and evolving as a photographer.

Mentor

Mr. Mohan Hathnapitiya

Highly reputed photographer & respected lecturer in Sri Lanka, whose guidance shaped my professional foundation.

mohan.hathnapitiya

After completing my school education, I decided to pursue my passion for photography by enrolling in a professional certificate course at Cameralk Academy the educational division of Cameralk , South Asia's largest and most renowned camera store. Cameralk Academy is known for its industry-standard training programs and is guided by some of the most experienced professionals in the field.

During this course, I had the privilege of studying under Mr. Mohan Hathnapitiya , a highly reputed photographer and respected lecturer in Sri Lanka. His extensive experience, artistic insight, and practical guidance played a significant role in shaping my skills and understanding of photography at a professional level.

Under his mentorship, I gained a solid foundation in both the technical and artistic aspects of photography. I learned how to operate and maintain various types of cameras, including DSLR and mirrorless systems, and developed skills in manual exposure control, focusing, and composition. I was also trained to critically "read" and analyze photographs—understanding lighting, framing, emotion, and visual storytelling. In addition, I studied photo editing techniques, learning to enhance images using professional software to bring out their full creative potential.

In addition to technical proficiency, the course emphasized client communication and professionalism. I learned how to work with clients effectively—understanding their vision, offering creative input, managing expectations, and delivering high-quality work on time.

Professional journey

Career & Vision

My journey in photography began during my Advanced Level (A/L) school years — a time when I was still discovering my creative voice. What started as a humble passion quickly transformed into a lifelong commitment to capturing the world through my lens. I began with simple birthday shoots and small-scale events organized by schools, community centers, and institutions. These early helped me develop a strong foundation in working with people, understanding lighting in diverse environments, and cultivating an intuitive eye for detail.

As I gained confidence and technical skills, I gradually advanced to more professional-level photography, including high-end birthday shoots, private parties, corporate events, and fashion modeling portfolios. Working with clients from different walks of life allowed me to refine my artistic vision while maintaining a strong focus on client satisfaction and storytelling through images.

In parallel to my client work, I also cultivated a deep personal passion for artistic and exploratory photography. I ventured into wildlife, landscape, and street photography, which allowed me to experience the world from new perspectives. Whether it was the raw intensity of animals in their natural habitat, the serene beauty of untouched landscapes, or the candid moments found in the chaos of urban life, each genre taught me something unique about patience, timing, and emotion.

Recognizing the importance of building an audience and sharing my work with the world, I established a presence across multiple social media platforms. My photography and videography content are consistently showcased on YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok, where I not only display finished work but also share behind-the-scenes footage, editing techniques, and creative processes. These platforms have enabled me to connect with a wide audience, collaborate with fellow creatives, and stay updated with global trends in photography and content creation.

In addition to content sharing, I also sell my photographs on Shutterstock and Adobe Stock , where my work reaches a global audience of photography enthusiasts, designers, and creative professionals. This experience has deepened my understanding of visual storytelling, market trends, and the business aspects of digital art.

Through years of hands-on experience, self-education, and client interaction, I've built a multifaceted career in photography that bridges the technical and artistic, the professional and the personal. Today, I continue to explore new styles and push creative boundaries, driven by the same passion that first inspired me during my school years.
