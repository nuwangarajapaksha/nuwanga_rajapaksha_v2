About Me

I'm Nuwanga

Rajapaksha

Hello! I’m Minimuthu Rajapaksha Pathirannahalage Nuwanga Tharuksha Rajapaksha, a 26-year-old Software Engineer, Photographer, Content Creator, Graphic Designer, Video Editor, Gym Enthusiastic and Traveler from Kelaniya, Sri Lanka. living at the crossroads of technology and creativity.

I studied at Nalanda College Colombo 10, where my passion for technology, creativity, and leadership first began to grow. I earned a Bachelor’s degree in Software Engineering from Birmingham City University and now work professionally as a Software Engineer, developing innovative solutions to real-world challenges.

While coding is my career, photography, graphic design and video editing are my creative outlets—hobbies that allow me to express emotion, capture beauty, and bring imagination to life in visual form.

Frames by Nuwanga
Photogrphy & Vediography

Every Frame

Tells a Story.

Photography found me before software did. My camera taught me patience, composition and the art of seeing the world differently. Today I work across landscape, street, wildlife, portrait, food and etc photography — always chasing the light, always hunting the decisive moment.

Beyond the stills, I produce cinematic films, travel vlogs and brand videos — editing with colour grades that breathe life into every pixel. And when the camera is away, I'm crafting graphic designs that tell brand stories through type, colour and composition.

Under Frames by Nuwanga, I share the shoots, the edits, the behind-the-scenes, the travels and the everyday moments that make life worth photographing.
