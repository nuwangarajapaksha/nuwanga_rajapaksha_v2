Gym & Strength

Strength Training & Conditioning

After my school journey, I began my gym journey with a deep passion and commitment to building a stronger and healthier version of myself. Rather than jumping straight into intense workouts, I dedicated the first two years purely to research and understanding the science behind fitness, nutrition, and bodybuilding. I studied different training methods, muscle groups, recovery techniques, and how the body responds to specific types of exercises. Over time, I developed my own personalized approach, based on how my physique responds best to training — a method that I continuously refine and improve.

Today, my gym routine is not just about lifting weights; it’s about precision, form, and progress. Some of my favorite exercises include the dumbbell bench press for building chest strength and stability, lat pull-downs and pull-ups to develop a strong and wide back, and seated dumbbell curls to isolate and grow the biceps. I also enjoy dumbbell lateral raises, which help shape the shoulders and enhance upper body symmetry. These exercises not only contribute to physical development but also teach me discipline, focus, and the importance of consistent effort. My gym journey has become a lifestyle — one that empowers me mentally and physically, and constantly pushes me to become the best version of myself.

Swimming & Flow

Endurance · Technique · Breath

Swimming is my meditation in motion. The moment I enter the water, the world above the surface fades away — replaced by the rhythmic sound of breath, stroke, and the gentle resistance of the pool. It is where I find complete mental clarity.

I focus on freestyle and backstroke, training with an emphasis on technique and efficiency rather than brute effort. Swimming has deepened my understanding of breathing — how controlling it under physical stress translates directly into performance, composure, and focus in every area of life.

The full-body nature of swimming, combined with its low-impact profile, makes it the perfect counterbalance to the heavy loads of the gym. It recovers the muscles while simultaneously building cardiovascular endurance and lung capacity.

Archery & Precision

Focus · Stillness · Accuracy

Archery is the sport of absolute stillness within motion. To release an arrow truly well, every external variable must be controlled — breathing, posture, grip, anchor point, and release must fuse into a single moment of perfect alignment. There are no second chances; the arrow reveals everything.

What drew me to archery was how profoundly it develops mental discipline. The act of drawing the bow, finding stillness amidst the tension, and committing fully to the release is a powerful metaphor for decision-making and execution under pressure — skills that translate far beyond the range.

I practice recurve archery, working on improving my groupings at various distances. Each session is a practice in mindfulness — a sport where the mind is as trained as the body.

Parachuting & Freedom

Courage · Trust · Euphoria

If the gym builds the body and archery sharpens the mind, parachuting tests the soul. The moment you stand at the open door of an aircraft, everything becomes crystalline — fear, exhilaration, and absolute presence merge into a single, irreversible commitment. There is no hesitation at that doorway.

Parachuting has profoundly shaped how I approach risk. In engineering, in life, and in sport, we often face moments where hesitation is more dangerous than action. The sky teaches you to trust your training, your preparation, and your gear — and then to commit fully.

The freefall is approximately 60 seconds of unfiltered reality — wind, silence, speed, and an indescribable feeling of freedom. Under the canopy, the world returns slowly, quietly, and beautifully. It is the most alive I ever feel.

Safety First, Always

Every jump is performed under certified instruction with full equipment checks, proper training, and within established safety protocols. The thrill of parachuting is inseparable from the discipline of doing it right.
